Forces change the motion of an object. A push or pull can make it speed up, slow down, or change direction.

Forces and Motion

A force is a push or pull that changes motion.

Motion: movement of an object (speed, velocity, acceleration).

Example: Kicking a ball changes its motion.

Energy

Energy makes things happen.

Kinetic:

moving energy (running)

Potential:

stored energy (stretched rubber band)

note:Heat, light, sound are other forms of energy.

Light and Sound

Light: can reflect (mirror) or refract (bending in water). Sound: travels in waves through air or water.
